# Book Fit Assessment Rules

## High fit
- entrepreneurship
- product
- writing
- sales
- negotiation
- management
- decision making
- growth
- course design

## Medium fit
- business biography
- case-driven books
- framework-heavy books

## Low fit
- novels
- pure literature
- abstract philosophy with low direct actionability

## Output requirements
- fit level
- reasons
- suggested skill count
- should proceed to plugin packaging or not
